About Us

Welcome to Canadian STEAKOUT, Sports Bar and Grill! We are fiercely independent, priding ourselves on simple, honest, modern Canadian food. Obsessively, we seek the best, most mouth-watering ingredients we can find locally from farmers, growers and markets, fusing them together to create innovative flavors and fresh, sumptuous dishes; putting our passion for your palate on a plate! Now, don’t let the words “sports bar” fool you! We are a family-friendly restaurant; the little ones are welcome to join us! Also, the food at Canadian Stakeout takes anything but your typical “on the line” food. The menu is so varied that you would have everything from Starters, Healthy Choices such as your greens and salads, our must tries – Asian Rice Bowls, Classic Pastas, Burgers and Fries, Fall-of-the-bone BBQ Ribs and, of course, the star of our show - STEAKS! But, if you are looking for the best bar food and drinks in town, Canadian Steakout is definitely the place for you! The burgers are award-winning and we offer a variety of flavors for our wings! All of the beers, liquors and wines, either permanent or seasonal, are specially handpicked from local and regional sources to perfectly satisfy everyone! Lastly, we pride ourselves with top notch customer service - we treat everyone as family! So, come and dine with us, if you want to have a good time and EXPERIENCE THE BEST!
